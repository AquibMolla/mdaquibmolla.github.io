About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Physics at the University of Calcutta. My research focuses on stochastic processes in complex systems, with particular interests in nonequilibrium statistical physics, first-passage phenomena, active matter, foraging dynamics, quantum walks, and transport in evolving environments.
