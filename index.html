About Me

I am a Doctoral researcher in Physics at Vidyasagar College under University of Calcutta, working in nonequilibrium statistical physics with a growing focus on active matter. My research investigates how stochastic dynamics, environmental feedback, and resource constraints shape transport, search and collective behavior in complex systems.

I combine analytical theory, stochastic modeling and numerical simulations to study active particles, random walks, foraging dynamics, first-passage processes and transport in evolving environments. My goal is to uncover the principles underlying emergence, adaptation and self-organization in nonequilibrium systems.

I welcome opportunities for collaboration, scientific discussion, and interdisciplinary research. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect!
